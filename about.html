About Victor Wood Products

A Baltimore institution in wood packaging — built on integrity, reliability, and craftsmanship for over 27 years.

Our Story

Victor Wood Products was founded in 1997 in Baltimore, Maryland — and we've been building wood pallets, custom crates, and industrial lumber products ever since. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the area's most trusted wood packaging manufacturers, serving freight forwarders, manufacturers, warehouses, and port businesses throughout the Baltimore region.

We are located at 7700 Rolling Mill Rd in Dundalk — just minutes from the Port of Baltimore and TradePoint Atlantic. That proximity isn't a coincidence. We built our business around the needs of the port economy: fast turnarounds, ISPM-15 compliant products, and the kind of reliable service that keeps supply chains moving.

Today, we are best known for our custom crating capabilities. When you have high-value machinery, industrial equipment, or specialized cargo that needs to move safely — domestically or internationally — we design and build the crate around your requirements. Every crate is built in-house, by our team, at our Baltimore facility.
