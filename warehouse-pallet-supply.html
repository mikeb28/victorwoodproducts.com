A Pallet Supplier That Shows Up

Warehouses and distribution centers run on pallets. When your supplier misses a delivery or ships substandard product, your operation feels it immediately. Victor Wood Products has been supplying pallets to Baltimore-area businesses since 1997 — and we've built our reputation on showing up reliably with quality product.

We manufacture all pallets in-house at our Dundalk facility at 7700 Rolling Mill Rd — no middlemen, no sourcing from unknown secondary suppliers. You get new pallets, built to your specification, delivered on your schedule.

Whether you need a standing weekly order for standard 48x40 pallets or a custom size that fits your racking system, we build to your requirements and deliver locally throughout Baltimore, Dundalk, Sparrows Point, Essex, and southeastern Baltimore County.
